Rice Pudding

Go back to Odin Recipes

Description

Simple and fast way to enjoy rice pudding.

Super yummy recipe to try once this winter!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • 1lt of Milk
  • ½ cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Fill a large pot with milk, sugar and cinnamon. Put a low fire and stir till the milk start smoking, then add the rice and keep stiring
  3. When the rice get thick turn off the fire and let it rest 10 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy your rice pudding!
